SpringGate E-Commerce carried the venue beyond the property line. It gave customers a simple way to bring local wines, beers, spirits, and seasonal releases home while keeping the purchase experience rooted in a place they already knew.

The storefront had to feel more personal than a generic checkout and more dependable than a small-business workaround. It needed to help customers browse confidently, understand fulfillment clearly, and move from discovery to order without friction.

## What We Did

- Presented SpringGate's catalog as a curated local shopping experience instead of a flat product list
- Made pickup, delivery, and ordering expectations clear at the moments customers needed them
- Helped shoppers compare wines, beers, spirits, and seasonal products quickly
- Created a smoother path from product discovery to checkout across device sizes
- Supported repeat customers with account-friendly shopping and familiar purchase patterns
- Extended the hospitality of the venue into a practical at-home ordering flow

## How It Worked

The shop balanced clarity with character. Product browsing stayed organized and direct, while the surrounding experience kept the sense of buying from a local destination rather than an anonymous inventory system. Customers could act quickly without losing the brand's warmth.

## Result

SpringGate gained a cleaner way to sell beyond the tasting room and event grounds. Customers gained a convenient path to order from a local brand they trusted, with the ease they expected from modern e-commerce and the familiarity they associated with visiting in person.
